Are You Experiencing These Symptoms? You Might Have an Infection!

Infections can affect different parts of the body, causing discomfort and health problems. If you are experiencing any of the symptoms below, you may have an infection that needs urgent treatment.

Common Symptoms of Infections

  • ✔ Fever or chills
  • ✔ Body weakness and fatigue
  • ✔ Skin rashes or itching
  • ✔ Pain or swelling in affected areas
  • ✔ Headaches and dizziness
  • ✔ Loss of appetite
  • ✔ Unusual discharge (for certain infections)
  • ✔ Persistent cough or sore throat
  • ✔ Stomach pain, diarrhea, or nausea

Types of Infections and Their Symptoms

Bacterial Infections

Fever, swelling, and redness in the affected area

Pus formation (for skin infections)

Cough with mucus (for respiratory infections)

Viral Infections

Fever, fatigue, and muscle aches

Sore throat and runny nose (common cold, flu)

Skin rashes (e.g., chickenpox, measles)

Fungal Infections

Itchy, scaly skin (ringworm, athlete's foot)

White patches in the mouth (oral thrush)

Thickened, discolored nails (nail fungus)

Parasitic Infections

Stomach pain, diarrhea, and weight loss (intestinal parasites)

Skin irritation and itching (scabies, lice)

Sexually Transmitted Infections (STIs)

Painful urination and genital sores

Unusual discharge and foul smell

Lower abdominal pain
